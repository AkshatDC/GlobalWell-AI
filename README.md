🌍 GlobalWell AI — Your AI Wellness Companion
Empowering accessible & affordable well-being aligned with UN SDG 3: Good Health and Well-being.

GlobalWell AI is a personalized wellness assistant that crafts actionable health & wellness plans based on user profiles, all while ensuring solutions are affordable, locally available, and holistic. Powered by Google's Gemini AI and an elegant Streamlit frontend, it provides users with tailored fitness, nutrition, mental well-being, and lifestyle improvement strategies.

✨ Features
  Personalized Wellness Plans — AI-generated health plans tailored to your profile & goals.

  Local Produce Insights — Hyper-local guides for seasonal, affordable fruits & veggies.

  Wellness Myth Busters — Evidence-based myth vs. fact sections for smart health choices.

  Mental Well-being Tools — Breathing exercises to promote mindfulness & relaxation.

  PDF Download — Export your custom wellness plan as a beautifully formatted PDF.

  Interactive Chat — Ask AI follow-up questions about your health plan in real-time.

  Dark Mode Toggle — Sleek light & dark themes for enhanced UX.

  Aligned with UN SDG 3 — All recommendations prioritize affordability, accessibility & holistic care.
